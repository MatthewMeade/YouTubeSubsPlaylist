Privacy Policy

Subscriptions Playlist Tool's use of information recieved from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

The application will only use access to your Google account to read a list of channels you are subscribed to, and populate a playlist on your YouTube account with videos recently uploaded by those channels.

The application does not collect or store any personal information related to you or your Google account. The app runs entirely in your browser.
